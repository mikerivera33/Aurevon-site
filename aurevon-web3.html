Web3 101

What Are NFTs — Really?

Forget the hype. Forget the monkey pictures. Here's what NFTs actually are and why they matter for real-world business.

🔑
Digital Proof of Ownership

An NFT (Non-Fungible Token) is a unique digital certificate recorded on a blockchain that proves you own something — an asset, a membership, a license. Unlike a screenshot, it can't be faked, duplicated, or revoked without your consent. Think of it as a digital deed to a house, except it works for anything.

🧱
Built on Blockchain

Blockchain is a public ledger that no single entity controls. When your Aurevon Shield is minted on Ethereum, that record exists forever — verifiable by anyone, owned only by you. No middleman. No company can "turn it off." Your membership exists independent of any server, database, or subscription.

🎫
More Than Art

The first wave of NFTs was digital art. The next wave is utility. Aurevon NFTs aren't collectibles — they're access tokens. Holding one unlocks tier-specific Discord channels, priority service, community perks, and a network of operators. The art is the shield. The value is what it unlocks.

💎
Scarcity = Value

"Non-fungible" means one-of-a-kind. A dollar is fungible — any dollar replaces any other dollar. An NFT is unique. Each Aurevon Shield has a tier, an edition number, and a provenance trail. As the community grows, the earliest shields become rarer. First movers are rewarded.

🔄
Transferable & Tradeable

Unlike a gym membership or a SaaS login, your NFT is yours. You can sell it. Gift it. Transfer it. If you cancel your Aurevon subscription, you keep the NFT. If the community grows and demand rises, your shield may appreciate. You own the asset, not just the access.

🌍
The Future Is On-Chain

Real estate deeds, business licenses, concert tickets, insurance claims — all of these will move on-chain within the next decade. Learning to use NFTs now puts you ahead of 99% of business operators. Aurevon is your entry point — start with what you already understand (real estate) and bridge into what's next.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a crypto wallet to use Aurevon services?
No. You can purchase any Aurevon Capital tier with a regular credit card via Stripe or PayPal. The NFT is a bonus that comes with your purchase. When you're ready to claim it, we'll walk you through setting up a free wallet (takes 2 minutes). The underwriting service works independently of the NFT.
What blockchain are Aurevon NFTs on?
Aurevon Shields are minted on Base (ERC-721), Coinbase's Ethereum L2 — giving you Ethereum-grade security at near-zero gas fees (~$0.01 per mint). Base is fully secured by Ethereum mainnet, interoperable with all major wallets and marketplaces (OpenSea, Blur, etc.), and used by Coinbase, Aerodrome, and hundreds of institutional projects. Your NFT is a real on-chain asset backed by the most established smart contract ecosystem.
What happens if I cancel my subscription?
You keep your NFT. It's yours forever — it lives in your wallet, not on our servers. However, your Discord tier role will be downgraded to match your active subscription level. If you resubscribe later, your role is restored instantly based on your NFT + active plan.
Can I sell or transfer my NFT?
Yes. Your Aurevon Shield is a standard ERC-721 token. You can sell it on OpenSea, transfer it to another wallet, or gift it. The new holder receives the NFT and its associated community access. However, service-tier benefits (like monthly deal allocations) are tied to your account, not the NFT alone.
How is this different from a regular membership?
Three ways: (1) You own it — no company can revoke your NFT. (2) It's verifiable — anyone can confirm you're a real Aurevon holder on-chain. (3) It may appreciate — as the community grows and early shields become rarer, demand may increase. A regular membership has none of these properties.
Is this a security or investment?
No. Aurevon NFTs are utility tokens — access passes to community features and service perks. They are not securities, do not represent equity in any company, and do not carry any expectation of profit. Any secondary market value is determined by open market dynamics, not by Aurevon.
What if I don't understand blockchain at all?
Perfect. That's exactly who Aurevon Labs is for. We built this to be the entry point for business operators — people who understand real estate, not Solidity. We handle the minting. We walk you through the wallet. You show up, do deals, and participate in the community. The blockchain part is invisible unless you want to learn more.
What's the difference between the Community Member NFT and the underwriting NFTs?
The Community Member NFT ($29.99/mo or $349.99 lifetime) gives you full Discord access and networking — the same community that underwriting clients get — but without any underwriting services. It's built for Web3 developers, entrepreneurs, investors, and anyone who wants to network and build in the Aurevon ecosystem without needing deal analysis. If you also need underwriting, purchase a Aurevon Capital package instead — it includes both the community access and the underwriting deliverables.
