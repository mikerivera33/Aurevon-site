Service Overview

Three analyses. One decision-ready report.

Every engagement ships a standardized deliverable built for operators, sponsors, and lenders who need institutional quality without institutional overhead.

MARKET

Submarket conditions, rent trends, demand drivers, vacancy, comparable properties, and local context for the subject asset.

DEAL

Acquisition assumptions, revenue, expenses, NOI, cap rate, DSCR, leverage, pro forma logic, and scenario testing.

RISK

Financing risk, lease-up risk, expense inflation, tenant/occupancy risk, and downside cases when assumptions weaken.

Deliverables

What lands in your inbox.

1

4

Quality Control

Every report carries labeled assumptions, missing-data flags, base/upside/downside commentary, a plain-English disclaimer, and human review before delivery.

FAQ

Common questions.

How fast is turnaround?

Standard delivery is 24–48 hours after payment and deal submission. Need it faster? The 12-hour rush add-on is $99 per deal — or included free with Pro Retainer and Enterprise tiers.

What's the difference between Second Opinion and Full Package?

Second Opinion ($189.99) includes two documents: an XLS/PDF underwriting worksheet and a 5-page investment analysis. Full Package ($250) includes both of those plus a synthesis worksheet with equity splits, refi/mortgage schedules, and a 5-year projection outlook. Full Package also includes your Tier 1 Aurevon Insider NFT.

What do I need to submit?

At minimum: property address, purchase price, rent roll, T12 or trailing expenses, and debt assumptions. After payment goes through, you'll be redirected to submit all your deal details. If you're missing pieces, we flag estimated values and label every assumption in the report.

How do payments work?

Pay with card via Stripe or use PayPal. Once your payment clears, you're immediately redirected to the deal intake form to submit your property details. Second Opinion and Full Package are one-time charges per deal. Pro Retainer and Enterprise are monthly subscriptions — cancel anytime.

What is the Aurevon Insider NFT?

It's a digital membership card delivered to your email — no crypto wallet needed. It proves your tier, grants access to our private Discord community, and unlocks holder benefits. Full Package = Aurevon Insider, Pro Retainer = Aurevon Ember, Enterprise = Aurevon Obsidian Executive. (Second Opinion at $189.99 does not include an NFT.)

Can you white-label reports for my firm?

Yes. White-label reports are a $175/deal add-on — or included free with Enterprise. Your branding, your cover page, delivered as if your team built it.

Are Aurevon Capital reports investment advice?

No. Reports are analysis support — a structured, reviewed view of the deal. They do not constitute investment, tax, or legal advice. Clients remain responsible for their own decisions.
